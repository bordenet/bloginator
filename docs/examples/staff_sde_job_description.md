# Staff Software Engineer

## About the Role

Staff Software Engineers (Level 4) are technical leaders who drive multi-team initiatives and establish technical direction across significant portions of our engineering organization. You'll operate at the intersection of deep technical expertise and organizational influence, solving complex problems that span multiple systems and teams.

This role requires 8+ years of software engineering experience with a proven track record of technical leadership, system design, and mentoring engineers across multiple teams.

## What You'll Do

### Technical Leadership

- Design and implement systems that span multiple teams and services
- Drive technical decisions that affect multiple parts of the organization
- Identify and solve technical problems before they become critical issues
- Establish engineering standards and best practices across teams
- Lead architecture reviews and provide guidance on complex technical decisions
- Build systems that are maintainable, scalable, and aligned with business needs

### Organizational Impact

- Influence technical direction across multiple teams
- Partner with engineering managers and product leaders on roadmap planning
- Identify low-risk, easily undoable actions and pursue them to completion
- Drive initiatives that improve engineering productivity and quality
- Build consensus across teams on technical approaches and priorities
- Translate business requirements into technical strategy

### Developing Others

- Mentor engineers across multiple teams, not just your immediate team
- Attract top talent through recruiting and technical interviews
- Provide meaningful, insightful code reviews that elevate team standards
- Share knowledge through documentation, tech talks, and pairing sessions
- Help engineers at all levels grow their technical and leadership skills
- Build technical leadership capacity across the organization

## What We're Looking For

### Experience and Background

- 8+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Track record of designing and building distributed systems at scale
- Experience leading technical initiatives across multiple teams
- History of mentoring and developing other engineers
- Demonstrated ability to drive technical decisions in ambiguous situations

### Technical Competencies

- Expert-level proficiency in at least one programming language and ecosystem
- Deep understanding of distributed systems, scalability, and reliability
- Strong system design skills with focus on maintainability and operability
- Experience with cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Solid grasp of data structures, algorithms, and performance optimization
- Ability to evaluate tradeoffs and make pragmatic technical decisions

### Leadership and Influence

- Proven ability to build consensus across teams and organizations
- Track record of driving technical initiatives from concept to completion
- Experience establishing engineering standards and best practices
- Ability to communicate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences
- Comfortable operating in ambiguous situations with incomplete information
- History of identifying and solving problems proactively

### Mindset and Behaviors

- Fortitude: Persistence in solving hard problems over weeks or months
- Growth mindset: Continuous learning and adaptation to new technologies
- Ownership: Taking responsibility for outcomes, not just outputs
- Pragmatism: Balancing ideal solutions with business constraints
- Collaboration: Working effectively across teams and disciplines
- Communication: Clear, concise writing and speaking

## How We Evaluate

We use the Four Quadrants framework to assess candidates:

**Company**: Understanding business context, cost management, and organizational impact. Staff engineers must connect technical decisions to business outcomes and operate with awareness of resource constraints.

**Core Skills**: Technical depth and breadth across systems, languages, and tools. Staff engineers are experts in their domain with strong fundamentals across the stack.

**Mindset**: Fortitude, growth orientation, and handling ambiguity. Staff engineers persist through complex problems and adapt to changing requirements.

**Behaviors**: Teamwork, communication, and influence. Staff engineers build consensus, mentor others, and elevate team performance.

## What Success Looks Like

### First 90 Days

- Understand our systems, architecture, and technical challenges
- Build relationships with engineers and leaders across teams
- Identify 2-3 high-impact technical improvements
- Begin contributing to architecture discussions and code reviews
- Establish credibility through technical contributions

### First Year

- Lead at least one multi-team technical initiative from design to delivery
- Mentor 3+ engineers across different teams
- Establish or improve engineering standards in your area
- Build trust across the organization as a technical leader
- Contribute to recruiting by conducting technical interviews

### Ongoing

- Drive technical strategy for significant portions of our platform
- Develop other engineers into technical leaders
- Identify and solve organizational-level technical problems
- Maintain deep technical expertise while expanding breadth
- Balance hands-on technical work with leadership and mentoring

## Engineering Culture and Values

We value engineers who:

- Write clear, maintainable code that others can understand and modify
- Document decisions and share knowledge proactively
- Seek feedback early and iterate based on input
- Focus on outcomes and impact, not just activity
- Take ownership of problems, even when they cross team boundaries
- Communicate clearly and concisely in writing and conversation
- Build systems that are operationally excellent and cost-effective

## Compensation and Benefits

We offer competitive compensation including base salary, equity, and comprehensive benefits. Compensation is determined based on experience, location, and market data.
