# Principal Software Engineer

## About the Role

We're seeking a Principal Software Engineer to drive technical strategy and execution across multiple teams. This role operates at the intersection of deep technical expertise and organizational influence, setting technical direction for company-wide initiatives while building engineering culture and developing others.

As a Principal Engineer, you'll solve previously unsolved problems, define engineering standards and best practices, and influence product and business strategy through technical insight. You'll represent engineering in executive discussions and mentor Staff engineers and engineering managers.

## What You'll Do

### Technical Leadership

- **Drive company-wide technical initiatives** with measurable impact on engineering velocity, system reliability, or product capabilities
- **Define engineering standards, patterns, and best practices** that scale across the organization
- **Solve ambiguous technical problems** that require novel approaches or advance the state of the art
- **Set technical direction** for major systems and platforms affecting multiple teams
- **Build consensus** on complex technical decisions across engineering and product leadership

### Organizational Impact

- **Influence product roadmap and business strategy** by identifying technical opportunities and constraints
- **Build and scale engineering culture** through mentorship, recruiting, and setting the technical bar
- **Partner with engineering managers** on team development, organizational design, and hiring strategy
- **Represent engineering** in executive discussions on strategy, investment, and priorities
- **Attract top talent** through recruiting, interviewing, and building the company's technical reputation

### Developing Others

- **Mentor Staff engineers and engineering managers** on technical leadership, decision-making, and career growth
- **Sponsor engineers for promotion** by creating growth opportunities and providing visibility
- **Build technical capabilities** across teams through knowledge sharing, documentation, and training
- **Interview candidates** for senior and principal roles, maintaining high hiring standards

## What We're Looking For

### Experience and Background

- **12+ years of software engineering experience** with demonstrated progression to senior technical leadership
- **Track record of driving technical initiatives** with company-wide or industry-wide impact
- **Deep expertise** in distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, or other relevant technical domains
- **Experience influencing** product and business strategy through technical insight
- **History of building and scaling** engineering practices, culture, or organizations

### Technical Competencies

- **Architectural vision**: Design systems that scale to millions of users or petabytes of data
- **Technical depth**: Expert-level knowledge in multiple technical domains with ability to go deep when needed
- **Problem-solving**: Decompose ambiguous problems into actionable plans with clear success criteria
- **Technical judgment**: Make sound decisions balancing technical excellence, business needs, and pragmatic constraints
- **Operational excellence**: Build systems with measurable reliability, performance, and cost efficiency

### Leadership and Influence

- **Build consensus**: Navigate complex technical and organizational dynamics to drive decisions
- **Earn trust**: Establish credibility across engineering, product, and business leadership
- **Communicate effectively**: Translate technical concepts for diverse audiences from engineers to executives
- **Develop others**: Grow technical capabilities across the organization through mentorship and knowledge sharing
- **Drive results**: Execute on multi-quarter initiatives with measurable outcomes

### Mindset and Behaviors

- **Fortitude**: Persist through ambiguity, setbacks, and organizational complexity
- **Growth mindset**: Continuously learn new technologies, domains, and leadership skills
- **Handle ambiguity**: Thrive in situations with incomplete information or unclear requirements
- **Ownership**: Take responsibility for outcomes beyond your direct control
- **Collaboration**: Partner effectively across functions and levels

## How We Evaluate

Performance is assessed across four interconnected quadrants:

**Company**: Understanding business context, managing cost and efficiency, taking ownership of outcomes

**Core Skills**: Technical competencies, architectural vision, operational excellence, domain expertise

**Mindset**: Fortitude, growth mindset, handling ambiguity, continuous learning

**Behaviors**: Teamwork, communication, influence, developing others

Excellence in one area cannot compensate for significant gaps in others. We look for balance across all quadrants.

## What Success Looks Like

### First 90 Days

- Build relationships with engineering leaders, product partners, and key stakeholders
- Understand current technical landscape, pain points, and opportunities
- Identify 1-2 high-impact technical initiatives aligned with business priorities
- Establish credibility through technical contributions and thoughtful feedback

### First Year

- Drive at least one company-wide technical initiative to measurable completion
- Influence engineering standards or practices adopted across multiple teams
- Mentor 2-3 Staff engineers or engineering managers with visible growth
- Contribute to product roadmap through technical insight and opportunity identification
- Build technical reputation internally and externally through talks, writing, or open source

### Ongoing

- Continuously elevate technical standards across the organization
- Solve problems that unlock new product capabilities or business opportunities
- Develop the next generation of technical leaders
- Shape engineering culture and practices as the company scales
- Represent the company's technical excellence externally

## Our Engineering Culture

We value:

- **Technical excellence** balanced with pragmatic delivery
- **Ownership and accountability** for outcomes, not just outputs
- **Continuous learning** and knowledge sharing
- **Collaboration** across teams and functions
- **Diversity of thought** and inclusive decision-making
- **Measurable impact** on customers, business, and engineering velocity
